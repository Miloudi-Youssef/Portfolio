RULES OF CLUEDO

Objective

Find out who committed the crime, with what weapon, and in which room.

Movement

Roll the dice to move across the board to enter rooms. Once inside a room, you can make a suggestion.

Suggestions & Accusations

Suggestion: Guess the suspect and weapon while in a room. Other players must secretly show you one card that disproves it if they have it.

Accusation: Once you are sure, make a final accusation. If you are right, you win! If wrong, you are eliminated.
